Music Interval Trainer Instructions

On this page you will hear musical intervals and be asked to identify them.

You may listen to an interval again by pressing the "Play Interval" button.

When you've made your choice click the "Submit" button to go on to a new interval.

At the end of the quiz your results will be displayed.

The following shorthand is used for interval identification:

  • P1 = Perfect Unison
  • min 2 = Minor Second
  • Maj 2 = Major Second
  • min 3 = Minor Third
  • Maj 3 = Major Third
  • P4 = Perfect Fourth
  • Tri-Tone = Augmented Fourth/Diminished Fifth
  • P5 = Perfect Fifth
  • min 6 = Minor Sixth
  • Maj 6 = Major Sixth
  • min 7 = Minor Seventh
  • Maj 7 = Major Seventh
  • P8 = Perfect Octave
